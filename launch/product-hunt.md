# Product Hunt

**Name:** Sera FX

**Tagline (60 chars max):**
Free real-time FX rates API — buy, sell & mid, no card

**Description:**
Sera FX is a free, real-time foreign-exchange rates API. Buy, sell and mid-market
quotes across 160+ currencies, refreshed every 60 seconds, historical data included.
No credit card, no paid tier, commercial use allowed.

The rates are live prices from Sera's on-chain FX settlement engine — so the same
quote you read can be settled on-chain in seconds. REST + JSON, SDKs for JS/Python/
PHP/Go, and an MCP server + OpenAPI 3 spec for AI agents.

We earn from the spread on real settlement flow, not from selling data. That's why
it's free, forever.

**First maker comment:**
Hey hunters 👋 We built Sera FX because everyone below enterprise pricing has been
stuck with stale, hobbled free FX tiers. Ours is the full feed — two-way quotes,
60-second refresh, history, agent-native — for $0. Would love your feedback,
especially from anyone building payments, invoicing, or AI finance agents.
